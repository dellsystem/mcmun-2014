Refund policy

The $75 club registration fee is a one-time non-refundable fee per delegation. If you decide not to join us at McMUN 2013:

  • Before November 18th, your refund will be the payment received minus the $75 club registration fee.
  • Before January 1st, your refund will be the payment received minus the deposit of {{ school.get_deposit }}.

Please note that after January 1st there are no refunds under any circumstances.

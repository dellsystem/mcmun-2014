Registering

International registration opening August 1

If your school is located outside of Canada and the United States, you'll be able to register through this website starting August 1st. All you'll have to do is fill out a short form and enter your billing details. We accept all major credit cards through PayPal as well as cheques.

North American registration opening September 1

American and Canadian schools can register starting September 1. Schools that know their Model UN schedule early in advance can take advantage of our reduced fee up until October 1. After October 1, our regular registration fee will apply, up until November 1. After November 1, schools will be added to the waitlist and will be accomodated on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Questions?

You can find out more about registration from our Payment policy page. Alternatively, feel free to contact our Chargée d'Affaires, Loreena Percy, at chargee@mcmun.org.
